I'M MAKING A LIST

Shel Silverstein

                I'm making a list of thing I must say
                            for politeness,
            And goodness and kindenss ande gentleness,
                        sweetness and rightness:
                            Hello
                            Pardon me
                            How are you?
                            Excuse me
                            Bless you
                            May I?
                            Thank you
                            Goodbye
                    If you know some that I've forgot,
                        please stick them in your eye!
