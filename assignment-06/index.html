Here Are 5 Fun Facts You May Not Know


  1. Around 30,000 rubber ducks were lost at sea in 1992.

   
            These ducks we included in a shipment from Hong Kong to the U.S. 
            One of the crates that were filled with rubber ducks was lost in the Pacific Ocean. 
            To this day, rubber ducks continue to pop up on the shores of Australia to Alaska.


  1. May 29th is Put a Pillow on Your Fridge Day.

            This odd holiday dates back to the early 1900s
            where families would place a piece of cloth on top of their larders.
            This is regularly celebrated in Europe and the U.S. to bring luck and wealth.


  1. You can’t hum while holding your nose.

            I bet you just tried it, didn’t you?


  1. There is an underwater version of rugby.

            Yes, it is also called underwater rugby. Two teams of 6 compete in a regulation pool while freediving.


  1. Lemurs were one the size of gorillas.

            They were a common sight before in Madagascar and used that land as its stomping grounds.
            This species of giant lemurs are now sadly extinct.
