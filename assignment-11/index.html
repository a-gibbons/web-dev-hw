Origins of ClueCluedo

The game was made by a British musician named Anthony Pratt. The game did originally come with 10 characters, 9 weapons, and 9 rooms.


The ten characters, now understandably have changed through the years, are listed as:

Rev Green (Mr. Green in North America). He is represented by a green token.
Colonel Yellow. He is represented by a yellow token.
Professor Plum. He is represented by a purple token.
Mrs. Peacock. She is represented by a blue token.
Nurse White. She is represented by a white token.
Mr Brown. He is represented by a brown token.
Mr Gold. He is represented by a gold token.
Miss Grey. She is represented by a grey token.
Mrs Silver. She is represented by a silver token.
Dr Black. He is represented by a black token.


The weapons, which are way more intense, are listed as:
Axe
Shillelagh (Walking stick)
Bomb
Rope
Dagger
Pistol
Hypodermic Syringe
Poison
Fireplace Poker
